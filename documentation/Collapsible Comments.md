# AO3: Collapsible Comments

Collapse and expand comment threads on AO3 works. Easily hide long threads for a cleaner reading experience.

---

## ‚ú® Features

- **Collapse/Expand Comments:** Tap any comment to hide or show its thread.
- **Collapse/Expand All:** Use the buttons after the comment pagination to collapse or expand all comments on the page.
- **Nested Threads:** Collapsing a parent comment hides all its replies.
- **Remembers State:** Collapsed comments stay hidden even after reloading or returning to the page.
