
# AO3: Auto Pseud

Auto-selects your pseud for comments and bookmarks on AO3, based on fandom.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Automatic Pseud Selection:**
  - When you comment or bookmark a work, the script will auto-select the pseud you’ve linked to that work’s fandom.
- **Configurable:**
  - Enable or disable auto-selection for comments and bookmarks independently.

---

## 📝 How to Use

1. **Edit Your Pseud:**
   - Go to your AO3 pseud edit page (`/users/USERNAME/pseuds/PSEUD/edit`).
   - Enter fandoms in the new field (comma-separated) and save.
   - Optionally, enable/disable auto-selection for comments and bookmarks.
2. **Comment or Bookmark a Work:**
   - When you open a comment or bookmark form, the script will auto-select the correct pseud if a fandom match is found.
   - You can still manually change the pseud if you wish.
